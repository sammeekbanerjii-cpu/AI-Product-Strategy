# The Prototype Bet

## What I Built
<!-- One sentence: what does this prototype demonstrate? -->

A working prototype of an AI Copilot that surfaces historical offer performance signals to help Salesforce marketing managers author and publish higher-converting offers faster.

## The Bet in One Sentence
<!-- What you're building, for whom, why now -->

-> If we surface 18 months of Salesforce.com offer performance signal at the point of authoring, marketing managers will ship better-converting offers faster — closing the data loop that currently sits dormant in Data Cloud.

## Kill Criteria
<!-- When would you stop? What evidence would kill this bet? -->

-> Marketers default to authoring offers manually and ignore AI recommendations after 60 days of availability
-> AI-generated drafts show no statistically significant lift in conversion score versus manually authored offers after two campaign cycles
-> Data Cloud signal quality proves insufficient to generate reliable recommendations — indicating the compounding data advantage is weaker than diagnosed
